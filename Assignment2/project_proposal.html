Title: Plants vs. Zombies

This website is all about Plants v.s. Zombies and its Sequels

Get to know more of the Plants v.s. Zombies Game and be immersed with the world of the game.

Target Audience

  • Everyone who loves the game and those who look forward to a new version
  • Kids - who wants to learn about the characters in a simplest way and on how to defeat them faster and plan their strategy of attack
  • Desktops/Laptops/ and Handheld owners who wants to know the minimum requirements to run this game on their devices

Objectives of this website

Primary Objective

Main objective of this site is to introduce the game, the characters either villain or hero and get familiar to their abilities and weaknesses , promote the different sequels of the game and to give and show tips on how to win this game.

This site will enable the player to move on to the next level with a limited resources but a better choice of each.
